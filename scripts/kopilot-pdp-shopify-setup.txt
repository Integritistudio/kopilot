Pricing model (single variant + quantity tiers)
-----------------------------------------------
The PDP uses ONE product with ONE variant. Tier selection sets cart quantity:

  Tier      Quantity    Display price    Compare-at
  --------  ----------  ---------------  ----------
  1 TAG     1           $39.99           —
  Set of 2  2           $71.99           $79.98 (Save 10%)
  Set of 3  3           $95.99           $119.97 (Save 20%)

Configure quantity / volume pricing in Shopify Admin:

1. Open the KOPILOT product in Admin.
2. Ensure the product has a single variant (e.g. "Default Title").
3. Set the base variant price to $39.99.
4. Go to Pricing → Quantity pricing (or Volume pricing, depending on your plan).
5. Add quantity breaks:
   - Minimum quantity 2 → price per unit or total that results in $71.99 for qty 2
   - Minimum quantity 3 → price per unit or total that results in $95.99 for qty 3

If quantity breaks are unavailable on your plan, alternatives:
  - Use Shopify Scripts / Functions for tiered discounts, or
  - Create three variants (1 / 2 / 3 tags) and update kopilot-pdp.js to add by variant ID instead of quantity.

Cart behavior
-------------
When a customer adds a different tier while the product is already in cart, the theme:
  1. Removes the existing line item for this product
  2. Adds a new line with the selected quantity

Template
--------
The default product template (templates/product.json) is the Kopilot PDP.
No per-product template assignment is required if this is your only product.
